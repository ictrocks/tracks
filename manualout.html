OUTPUT GUIDE

The PV system sizing and some of the components' specifications
are show here.

For Charge Controller

The user will need to find a charge controller with
Maximum Input Voltage larger than Vmax and
Maximum Input Current larger than Imax as well.

It is recommended to have a charge controller with Maximum Power
Point Tracking (MPPT) capability to ensure best performance.

This will allow the simultaneous use of the appliances
listed.
